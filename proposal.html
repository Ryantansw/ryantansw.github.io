Generic 1-Day Programme Flow

Customised to your objectives
  1. 8:45–9:15
    Arrival & Attendance
    Registration, settling in, and briefing on the day's flow.
  2. 9:15–10:00
    Opening & Contextualisation
    Workshop objectives, roles, and rules of engagement.
    Shared expectations and psychological safety.
  3. 10:00–10:30
    Ice Breaker & Team Challenge
    Challenge testing coordination and communication under pressure.
    Insights into roles, assumptions, and team habits.
  4. 10:30–12:15
    Values into Action
    Interactive exercise linking behaviours, performance, and norms.
    Draft team norms and working principles.
  5. 12:15–12:30
    Debrief & Reflection
    What's working, what's not, what needs to change.
    Agreed priorities and improvement themes.
  6. 12:30–1:30
    Lunch
    Break and informal connection.
  7. 1:30–2:30
    Know Yourself, Know Your Team
    Communication and working styles exercise.
    Empathy, fewer misunderstandings, clearer preferences.
  8. 2:30–3:30
    Synergy Challenge
    Problem-solving requiring teamwork and adaptability.
    Alignment, speed, escalation, and decision points.
  9. 3:30–3:45
    Tea Break
    Short break.
  10. 3:45–4:30
    Overcoming Challenges
    Handling pressure, setbacks, and change as one team.
    Tools and language for resilience.
  11. 4:30–5:15
    Transfer of Learning
    Debrief, reinforcement, and practical action planning.
  12. 5:15–5:30
    Final Debrief & Close
    Individual commitments and next steps.
    Post-programme review.
